# Cinematic Style Guide

## Vibe
Apple keynote cinematography · Vercel-style UI storytelling · Linear-quality motion graphics · Arc Browser transitions · Framer-inspired micro-interactions

## Camera Movements
- Slow push-ins
- Gentle pans
- Floating parallax
- Elegant rotations
- Depth-of-field focus shifts
- Smooth easing (cubic-bezier where possible)

## Effects
- Soft bloom
- Subtle reflections
- Animated gradients
- Glassmorphism (frosted glass)
- Volumetric lighting
- Premium light sweeps
- Floating UI layers
- Ambient particles

## Avoid
- Flashy neon
- Heavy glitches
- Aggressive camera shakes
- Over-the-top sci-fi effects
- Distracting visual noise
